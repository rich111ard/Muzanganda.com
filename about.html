About us

Muzanganda is dedicated to transforming our community by bridging gap between access and opportunity.
Our vision To close the digital inequity gap across all households- One Computer, One home.
Our mission To facilitate applied training and provide resources to empower individuals to meaningfully use technology to holistically improve their lives.

We offer IT classes in computer literacy and introduction to coding for children and youth. We support grant writing for community organizations and share scholarships information.

Join us in our commitment to foster growth and success in our community!
